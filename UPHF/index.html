I am a full professor at the Université Polytechnique Hauts-de-France, France, and a member of LAMIH. From 2008 to 2019, I was an associate professor at the University of Strasbourg, France, and a member of ICube Laboratory in the Network research group, and a visiting researcher at Inria Lille - Nord Europe, in the FUN research group (full 2-year secondment, 2017-19).

I hold a master (2004), a PhD degree (2007) in computer science from the University of Lille, and an HDR thesis (2017) from the University of Strasbourg.

My research topics include wireless ad hoc and mesh networks, actuator and sensor networks, Industrial Internet of Things, activity scheduling, routing and MAC protocols, mobile networks, fault-tolerance, cybersecurity and performance evaluation.
